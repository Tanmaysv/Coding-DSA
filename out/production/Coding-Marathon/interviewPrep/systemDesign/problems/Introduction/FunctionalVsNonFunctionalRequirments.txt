Functional Requirements:

1)This are generally provided by client/users
2)Basic input output
3)Defined at component level to verify functionality of system

Non-Functional Requirements:

1)This are quality contraints and SLAs
2)It deals with qualoty attribute of system
3)Applied to system as a whole

This are some example of Non-Functional Req:

Portability
Security
Maintainability
Reliability
Scalability
Performance
Reusability
Flexibility
